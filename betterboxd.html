Betterboxd Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

Betterboxd is a film tracking and discovery app project. This page explains, in simple terms, how personal information may be handled when using the app.

Information We May Collect

Betterboxd may collect information you choose to provide, such as account details, profile information, watchlists, ratings, reviews, and other in-app activity needed to provide the service.

How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to operate the app, save your preferences, show your film activity, improve reliability, and respond to support or privacy requests.

Sharing

Betterboxd does not sell personal information. Limited data may be processed by service providers that support hosting, analytics, crash reporting, or authentication when needed to run the app.

Data Security

Reasonable steps may be taken to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may request information about your data or ask privacy-related questions by using the contact details listed on the main portfolio page.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as Betterboxd develops. The latest version will be posted on this page.
