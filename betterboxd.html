Betterboxd Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

Betterboxd is a film tracking and discovery app project. This page explains, in simple terms, the app's privacy approach.

Data Collection

Betterboxd does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. The app does not require user accounts and does not gather names, email addresses, device identifiers, location data, usage analytics, or film activity data.

How Data Is Used

Because Betterboxd does not collect data, there is no personal information to use, analyze, or profile.

Sharing

Betterboxd does not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties because no personal data is collected.

Tracking and Analytics

Betterboxd does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or similar monitoring tools.

Your Choices

Since Betterboxd does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as Betterboxd develops. The latest version will be posted on this page.
