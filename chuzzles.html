Chuzzles Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Chuzzles is a free cross-platform chess puzzle app available on iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS. The app is designed for local puzzle play with different modi.

Data Collection

Chuzzles collects no data at all.

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No usage or gameplay data is collected.
  • No analytics data is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected.
  • No location data is collected.

Tracking and Advertising

Chuzzles uses no tracking technologies, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party analytics tools.

Accounts and Network Services

Chuzzles does not require user accounts and does not send data to external servers.

Data Sharing

Since Chuzzles collects no data, there is no data to sell, share, or disclose.

Policy Changes

If Chuzzles privacy practices change in the future, this page will be updated before those changes take effect.
